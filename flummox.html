How to play

Swipe — or use arrow keys — and every tile slides as far as it can go.

When 3 or more of a colour touch, they clear. Clears can chain.

Empty the board in as few swipes as possible. Match par — the proven minimum.

Careful: leave fewer than 3 of a colour and they're stranded for good. Undo costs a swipe.
