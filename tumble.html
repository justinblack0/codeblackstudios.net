How to play

Swipe (or use arrow keys) and every jelly slides as far as it can. When 3 or more of the same colour touch, they pop — and pops can chain. Clear the board in as few swipes as possible. Match par for the trophy. Careful: leave fewer than 3 of a colour and they're stranded for good.
